What are the payment methods and limits by which you operate?

Cryptex accepts Visa and Mastercard for credit/debit card transactions, with a minimum purchase of 10 USD and a transaction limit of 6000 USD. Card limits include 5 purchases per day, 10 purchases per week, and a daily/monthly purchase limit of 25000 USD.


For cryptocurrency transactions (Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, Zcash, Litecoin, Dogecoin), there are no limits on purchases, and transactions are unlimited for both daily and monthly periods.


Please note that while there are no specific limits for cryptocurrency transactions, certain banks may impose additional fees, known as Cash Advance fees, for cryptocurrency-related services. For further clarification or assistance, please contact our customer support.
